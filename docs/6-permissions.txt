Commands to do with giving users permission to send as your character.
,give-access me $user
    Lets another user send messages as your character. $user should be the full discord name e.g. naverag#7241.
    They send messages the same way as for NPCs, i.e. ,$name $message
,check-access me
    Lists who has access to send as your character.
,remove-access me $user
    Removes the permission for another user to send messages as your character.
