,avatar $avatar-url-or-image
    Sets your character's avatar. Also adds it as a switchable avatar with key default.
    $avatar-url-or-image can be a URL to an image or you can upload an image using Discord's upload function.
    If you use the latter, if the message is deleted then the avatar will stop working.
,avatar $key $avatar-url-or-image
    Sets a switchable avatar with key $key.
,switch-avatar $key
    Switches to one of your switchable avatars.
,list-avatars
    List your switchable avatars.
,remove-avatar $key
    Removes a switchable avatar. If it's your current avatar, clears your current avatar as well.
